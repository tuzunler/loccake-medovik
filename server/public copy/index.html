Our Story

Bringing Russian Baking
Traditions to You

Loccake Cafe was born from a grandmother's kitchen in Moscow, where every Sunday meant the heavenly aroma of fresh Medovik and the delicate layers of Napoleon filling the home.

Today, we continue those cherished traditions with the same dedication to quality and authenticity. Our recipes have been passed down through three generations — each cake is a love letter to Russian culinary heritage.

Every ingredient is carefully selected: real butter from local farms, organic honey from Russian apiaries, Belgian chocolate, and farm-fresh eggs. No shortcuts. No compromises.
