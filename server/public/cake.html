Medovik — Honey Cake

Медовик — The queen of Russian cakes since 1820

$42.00 starting price / 1.2 kg

Our Medovik is a masterpiece of patience and tradition. Each of the 12 ultra-thin honey-infused sponge layers is individually hand-rolled and baked, then assembled with a luxurious sour cream filling and refrigerated overnight. The result is a cake that melts on your tongue — honeyed, tender, and impossibly light.

This recipe has been in our family for three generations, originating from a small village bakery near Tula. We use only raw organic honey from Russian apiaries, farm-fresh eggs, and cultured sour cream. No shortcuts. No artificial anything.
